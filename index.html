What is Cyberbullying?

Cyberbullying is bullying that takes place over digital devices. It can happen via text, social media, gaming platforms and more - and its effects are just as real as in-person bullying.

Harassment

Repeatedly sending offensive or threatning messages to someone online

Exclusion

Intentionally leaving someone out of online groups or conversations to isolate them

Impersonation

Pretending to be someone else online to damage their reputation or relationships
